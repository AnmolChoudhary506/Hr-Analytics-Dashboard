# HR-ANALYTICS-DASHBOARD
1. Title
*  HR Analytics: Employee Attrition & Workforce Insights Dashboard

2. Description
   
   This HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard created to examine changes in salary across departments, workforce       demographics, and employee attrition.It assists management and HR teams in identifying high-risk personnel segments, factors
   contributing to turnover, and areas in need of strategic intervention.

4. Business Problem
   
   Employee attrition leads to increased hiring costs, productivity loss, and operational disruption.
   Organizations often lack a centralized, data-driven view to understand:

   * Why employees leave

   * Which departments and roles are most affected

   * How age, salary, tenure, gender, and education impact attrition

   * This dashboard addresses these gaps by providing actionable HR insights.


5. Goal of the Dashboard

   * Analyze overall and department-wise attrition

   * Identify high-risk employee groups

   * Understand attrition patterns by age, salary, tenure, gender, education, and job role

   * Support data-driven HR decision-making to reduce attrition

* Dataset: HR_Analytics.csv

* Data Type: Employee-level HR data

* Key Fields Include:

   * Department

   * Job Role

   * Attrition

   * Age

   * Gender

   * Education Field

   * Salary

   * Years at Company
     
7. Key KPIs (Displayed on Dashboard)

   Overall Metrics (by Department View)

   * Total Employees

   * Total Attrition

   * Attrition Rate (%)

   * Average Age

   * Average Salary

   * Average Years at Company

   These KPIs update dynamically based on department selection (HR, R&D, Sales).
